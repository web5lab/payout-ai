Wrapped Token Payout Distribution Flowchart

Start: User Buys Token from Offering Contract

User acquires tokens from a designated offering contract

Register Investment in Wrapped Token Contract

The `WrapedToken` contract records the user's deposited amount and mints wrapped tokens.

Original Tokens Locked in Wrapped Token Contract

The user's original deposited tokens are held in the `WrapedToken` contract until the maturity date, preventing transfers.

Offering Contract Initializes APY and Payout Period

The offering contract (or owner) initializes the APY and payout period duration in the `WrapedToken` contract during the public offering setup.

Offering Contract Deposits Payout Funds

The offering contract (or owner) deposits reward tokens into the `WrapedToken` contract for distribution.

Is Payout Enabled and Due?
NO (Not Enabled/Due)
Wait for Payout to be Enabled/Due

Payouts cannot be claimed until enabled by the offering contract and a payout period has passed.

YES (Enabled & Due)
User Initiates Claim Reward

User calls `claimReward()` on the `WrapedToken` contract.

Wrapped Token Contract Calculates Reward

The `WrapedToken` contract calculates the reward based on the user's deposited amount, APY, and payout period.

Are Funds Available and Claim Not Used for Period?
NO (Insufficient Funds/Already Claimed)
End: Claim Rejected

Claim is rejected due to insufficient funds in the contract or already claimed for the current period.

YES (Funds Available & Not Claimed)
Wrapped Token Contract Transfers Reward to User

The `WrapedToken` contract transfers the calculated reward tokens to the user's wallet.

End: User Successfully Claims Reward

The user receives their earned tokens, completing the payout cycle for the period.

Is Maturity Date Reached?
NO (Not Matured)
Continue Earning/Claiming Rewards

User can continue to earn and claim rewards in subsequent payout periods until maturity.

YES (Matured)
User Claims Final Tokens

User calls `claimFinalTokens()` to burn wrapped tokens and receive original deposited `peggedToken`.

End: Investment Cycle Complete

User has claimed all rewards and original investment.
